About Us

We help Lithuanian businesses reduce repetitive work and improve client communication through practical automation. Unlike large consultancies, we deliver fast, flexible solutions on top of the tools you already use — Google, CRMs, spreadsheets, and email.

Our mission is to make automation accessible to small and mid-sized businesses, enabling them to scale without the complexity of new systems.
